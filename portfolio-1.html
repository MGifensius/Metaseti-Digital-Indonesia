Logistics Landing Page

Harton Logistics, a leading freight and supply chain management company, needed a modern landing page to attract enterprise clients and streamline their lead generation process. The challenge was to communicate their comprehensive logistics solutions while establishing trust with businesses seeking reliable shipping partners.

The Challenge

Standing Out in a Competitive Market

Harton Logistics faced fierce competition in the freight forwarding industry. Despite offering superior service and competitive rates, their outdated web presence wasn't attracting the enterprise clients they targeted. They needed a landing page that would showcase their capabilities, build credibility, and generate qualified leads from businesses seeking comprehensive logistics solutions.

The main challenges included differentiating their services from competitors, communicating the complexity of their supply chain management offerings in simple terms, showcasing their global network and tracking capabilities, and building trust with potential clients who were entrusting their valuable cargo and time-sensitive shipments.

The Solution

Professional Design for Complex Services

We conducted extensive research into the logistics industry, analyzing competitor websites and interviewing Harton's target clients to understand what enterprise buyers look for when selecting a logistics partner. This informed our design strategy, emphasizing reliability, transparency, and global reach.

The landing page was built with a modular architecture using Next.js, allowing Harton to easily update service information and add new case studies. We integrated an interactive global map showcasing their network, a live shipment tracking demo, and an intelligent quote request form that qualifies leads based on shipping volume and requirements.

Every section was designed to address common concerns in the logistics industry - from highlighting certifications and compliance standards to showcasing successful delivery case studies. We implemented strategic CTAs throughout the journey, guiding visitors from service exploration to quote requests, ultimately positioning Harton as the trusted logistics partner for growing businesses.
