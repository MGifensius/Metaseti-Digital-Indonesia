The Challenge

Legacy Manufacturing Systems

Traditional factories operate with isolated machines and manual monitoring processes. Equipment failures cause unexpected downtime, maintenance is reactive rather than preventive, and production inefficiencies remain hidden. Managers lack real-time visibility into operations, leading to delayed responses and lost productivity. Manual data collection is error-prone and time-consuming.

The Solution

Connected Smart Factory

We deployed an comprehensive IoT infrastructure that connects every machine, sensor, and process on the factory floor. Real-time monitoring tracks temperature, vibration, energy consumption, and production output. AI algorithms predict equipment failures before they occur, automated alerts notify operators of anomalies, and centralized dashboards provide instant visibility across all operations, enabling proactive decision-making.

IoT Architecture

Three-Layer System

Sensor Network

Hundreds of IoT sensors monitor temperature, vibration, pressure, and energy consumption across the factory floor.

Edge Processing

Local gateways process data in real-time, enabling instant responses and reducing cloud bandwidth requirements.

Cloud Analytics

Centralized platform aggregates data for advanced analytics, machine learning, and long-term trend analysis.

Key Features

Industrial IoT Capabilities
